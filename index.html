About Me

I am a passionate UI/UX Designer with a background in Information Technology, dedicated to creating intuitive and user-centric designs. I specialize in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and crafting responsive interfaces that blend functionality with aesthetics. Proficient in tools like Figma and Adobe Photoshop, I excel at transforming complex ideas into visually compelling and accessible solutions. With a keen eye for detail and a collaborative approach, I work with cross-functional teams to understand user needs, solve design challenges, and deliver impactful experiences that align with business goals.
